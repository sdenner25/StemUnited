About Us

Introduction

We are a non profit organization founded by High School Robotics Mentors of the Feds 201. STEM United oversees high school, middle school and elementary level robotics teams.

Vision Statement

Empowering youth locally and globally by creating inclusive opportunities for all students to explore, engage, and excel in STEM education.

Mission Statement

STEM United is dedicated to expanding access to STEM education through community-based initiatives and international partnerships. We support and launch FIRST robotics programs, conduct STEM-focused workshops, and host outreach events to inspire learners of all ages. Our mission is to cultivate curiosity, confidence, and future careers in STEM by creating meaningful, hands-on learning experiences across the globe.
